# HEARTBEAT.md - What to check when you wake up

## Every heartbeat
1. Check email inbox for new unread messages
2. If anything important or time-sensitive, message Steve on Telegram
3. If nothing needs attention, reply HEARTBEAT_OK

## Rules
- Late night (23:00-08:00): HEARTBEAT_OK unless genuinely urgent
- Do not repeat notifications Steve has already seen
- Keep Telegram messages brief — subject line and one-line summary, not the full email
