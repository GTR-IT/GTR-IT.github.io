The problem: you're paying too much

If your business uses WordPress, there's a good chance you're paying a web agency £50 to £150 per month (or more) just to host and maintain your site. That's £600 to £1,800 a year for something that could cost a fraction of the price.

It's not that agencies are doing anything wrong. They bundle hosting, updates, and support into a monthly fee, and for many businesses that felt like the simplest option when the site was first set up. But over time, the costs stack up while the actual work involved rarely justifies the price tag.

The trouble is, most business owners don't realise there's an alternative. They assume that leaving their agency means losing their website, or that managing hosting themselves is too technical. So they keep paying, year after year, for something that costs a fraction of what they're being charged.

The solution: take back control

We migrate your existing WordPress site to a hosting account that you own. Not a reseller account. Not an agency middleman. Your own hosting, in your name, under your control.

The site stays exactly the same. Same design, same content, same functionality. It just lives somewhere far less expensive, and you hold the keys.

Common questions

Will my website look the same?

Yes. We migrate a complete copy of your existing WordPress site. Same theme, same content, same layout. Your visitors will not notice any difference.

What about updates and maintenance?

WordPress handles most core and plugin updates automatically. If you'd prefer a safety net, we can set up a maintenance plan to handle updates, backups, and security checks on your behalf.

Will I lose any functionality?

No. The entire site migrates as-is: plugins, themes, custom code, forms, e-commerce features. Everything that works now will work after migration.

How long does migration take?

Typically same day. Most straightforward WordPress sites are migrated and tested within a few hours. More complex setups may take a bit longer, and we'll always let you know upfront.

What if something goes wrong?

We test everything thoroughly before switching your domain over. Your existing site stays live throughout the process. If anything doesn't look right, we fix it before going live, not after.
