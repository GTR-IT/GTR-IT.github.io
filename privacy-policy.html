Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GTR IT respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we look after it, and what rights you have.

Who we are

GTR IT is a UK-based IT consultancy offering AI agents and workflow automation to small and medium-sized businesses. We're run by Steve Peacock (steve@gtr-it.co.uk).

For privacy questions, contact us at steve@gtr-it.co.uk.

What data we collect and why

When you contact us

If you email us or fill in a contact form on our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name (if provided)
  • Your message or enquiry

Why: To respond to your enquiry, discuss potential work, and follow up if needed.

When you become a client

During project work, we may collect:

  • Business contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Information about your systems, processes, and workflows (only what's needed for the project)
  • Files, documents, or data you share with us to complete the work
  • Payment information (invoices, billing address)

Why: To deliver the services you've asked for, manage the project, and handle invoicing and payments.

Website visitors

Our website does not currently use analytics, tracking cookies, or other monitoring tools. We don't collect data about your browsing activity or IP address.

How we store and protect your data

We keep your information secure:

  • Data is stored on secure, password-protected systems
  • Only Steve Peacock has access to your information
  • We do not share your data with third parties, except where required by law
  • All business files are stored locally or on secure, UK-based cloud services

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorised access, but no system is completely secure. If we discover a data breach, we'll contact you without unreasonable delay.

How long we keep your data

We keep your information for as long as needed to:

  • Client communications and project files: 6 years after the project ends (for accounting and tax purposes)
  • General enquiries: 1 year, unless you ask us to delete it sooner
  • Invoices and financial records: 7 years (UK tax law requirement)

After these periods, we delete or anonymise your data.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Ask what data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to fix incorrect information
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restrict processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Data portability: Ask for a copy of your data in a standard format
  • Withdraw consent: If you've consented to something, you can withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email steve@gtr-it.co.uk with "Data Subject Request" in the subject line. We'll respond within 30 days.

Third parties

We don't routinely share your data. However, we may share information when:

  • Required by law or a court order
  • You ask us to (for example, if you want us to work with another provider)
  • It's necessary to complete your project (e.g., with a payment processor for invoicing)

Any third parties we work with are bound by similar confidentiality and data protection commitments.

International transfers

GTR IT is UK-based and stores data in the UK. We don't routinely transfer data outside the UK or EU. If we need to, we'll use appropriate safeguards (like Standard Contractual Clauses) and tell you first.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make significant changes, we'll tell you by email or by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services means you accept the updated policy.

Questions or complaints

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, email steve@gtr-it.co.uk.

If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Phone: 0303 123 1113
